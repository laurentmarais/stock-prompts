# Momentum Stocks

Date: March 25, 2026

## Scope

This screen looked for liquid small-cap and mid-cap names showing early public signs of momentum before a broad breakout or full retail chase.

The main filters were:

- a credible near-term catalyst, usually earnings within the next 2 to 8 weeks,
- rising or above-normal volume,
- relative strength that is starting to improve,
- a setup that still looks early rather than already fully extended,
- no obvious dilution, going-concern, or promotion-driven red flags.

The opportunity set is thinner than it looked a day ago. A large share of the highest-volume names are either already stretched, tied to binary biotech or space headlines, or lack enough profitability and balance-sheet quality to qualify as clean early-momentum swing setups.

## Notes

- The cleanest current pattern is still quiet accumulation ahead of a dated catalyst, but there are fewer clean examples than yesterday.
- I excluded high-volume biotech, space, and story-stock names because the move depended too heavily on binary event risk or already-crowded attention.
- I kept some profitable names such as DIOD and ISSC on the watchlist because either volume expansion or tape confirmation is not strong enough yet.
- Options-flow confirmation was not required here because public tape behavior and catalyst timing were more reliable than chasing isolated options spikes.

## Method

- Started with a liquid small-cap and mid-cap universe using public market data.
- Narrowed to names with elevated recent volume, positive or stabilizing price action, and a credible near-term catalyst.
- Favored names that were not already fully vertical or obviously overexposed to promotional behavior.
- Used earnings timing, 5-day versus 20-day volume, 20-trading-day performance versus SPY, and distance from 3-month highs as the main public signals.
