## Bottom Line

The cleanest current early-momentum names are AMPY and ASC.

- AMPY is the better small-cap oil setup if crude stabilizes rather than collapses.
- ASC is the cleaner shipping setup if physical dislocation continues to outlast the market's ceasefire optimism.

Everything else I reviewed was either too illiquid, too obvious already, too dependent on a single macro headline, or not early enough to justify calling it a true pre-crowd momentum setup.
